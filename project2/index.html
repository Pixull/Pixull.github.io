Hi, I’m Greg, and I love photographing cats. There’s something magical about capturing a cat’s unique personality—whether they’re curious, playful, or a little sassy. My goal is to create photos that truly reflect who your cat is, so you can have lasting memories of your furry friend.I know cats can be unpredictable, and that’s part of what makes photographing them so fun. I keep my sessions relaxed and low-pressure, letting your cat set the pace. Whether they’re lounging in their favorite sunny spot or pouncing on a toy, I’ll be there to capture those genuine moments. Let’s work together to create photos that celebrate everything you love about your cat.
